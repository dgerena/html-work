Things to think about before deciding what to bring.

  • Weather conditions in your field area. Field locations vary to some degree but being prepared for swamp conditions or dust storms will ensure a much less stressful environment.
  • Your units PPE standards may vary from location. Check beforehand to stay ahead of the game.
  • Include enough of soilable items to last the time alloted for your field op. Even if able to wash less time wasted the better.
