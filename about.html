Equipment sources.

  • Minimum for US forces.
  • Military desert training.
  • Winter training.

Checking your gear.

  1. Inventory your items by laying them out visibly.
  2. Check for scratches or cracks in any equipment. If any are found replace if capable. Otherwise inform your NCO or CO.
  3. Identify any equipement that will need to be readily available.
  4. Place said items nearest to the top of rucksack or its pouches.
  5. Ensure you have packed all items as tightly as possible to minimize wasted space or noise.

Things to think about before deciding what to bring.

  • Weather conditions in your field area. Field locations vary to some degree but being prepared for swamp conditions or dust storms will ensure a much less stressful environment.
  • Your units PPE standards may vary from location. Check beforehand to stay ahead of the game.
  • Include enough of soilable items to last the time alloted for your field op. Even if able to wash less time wasted the better.
